# Pula Trip Companion 🏛️🏖️

A single-file, **offline** trip guide for your Pula getaway — **Thu 9 → Tue 14 July 2026**, built for two.

Everything lives in **`index.html`**. No internet, app store, or account needed. Open it, add it to your home screen, and it works with zero data while you're there.

---

## 📲 Put it on your phone (do this before you fly)

The app is one file, so the trick is just getting `index.html` onto your phone and saving it to the home screen.

### Easiest: open it, then "Add to Home Screen"
1. Get `index.html` onto your phone — e.g. email it to yourself, drop it in your cloud drive (iCloud/Google Drive), or AirDrop it, then **open it in your browser**.
2. **iPhone (Safari):** tap the **Share** button → **Add to Home Screen** → *Add*.
3. **Android (Chrome):** tap the **⋮** menu → **Add to Home screen** → *Add*.

Now there's a **Pula Trip** icon on your home screen that opens full-screen like an app — and it keeps working **offline**.

> Tip: On iPhone, opening the file straight from the **Files** app also works offline. On Android, the **Chrome** browser handles local files most reliably.

## ✨ What's inside

- **🏠 Today** — knows the date: shows a countdown before the trip, then *today's* plan while you're there.
- **🗓️ Days** — the full relaxed day-by-day itinerary (tap any day to expand; tick things off as you go).
- **🧭 Explore** — *Getting around* (your Palisina bus stop + Line 3), *Beaches*, *Eat & drink*, and the *Film Festival*.
- **💶 Budget** — an editable cost breakdown for two; change any number and it re-totals. Toggle the Brijuni splurge on/off.
- **ℹ️ Info** — practical tips, emergency numbers, handy Croatian phrases, and a packing checklist.

Extras: **dark mode** (🌙 top-right), **"Open in Maps"** buttons on every place, and your ticks/budget/theme are **saved on your phone**.

---

## 🔒 Offline & privacy

- Works with **no internet** — all text, maps schematic and logic are built in.
- **"Open in Maps"** links need data/Wi-Fi (they hand off to Google Maps); everything else does not.
- Nothing is uploaded anywhere. Your checklist ticks and budget edits are stored **only in your browser** (localStorage) on your device.
